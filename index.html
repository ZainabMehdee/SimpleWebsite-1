Task Timeline

Task timelines are essential to ensuring that your project is transparent and well-planned.


  • Understand the scope of your project.

  • Begin breaking the project down into smaller pieces.

  • Explore those pieces to create tasks.

  • Calculate the time required for each task.

  • Make a task timeline.

  • Do all the tasks one by one until they are all done.
